ADR - stands for Architecture Decision Record.
    An ADR is a short document that answers one question:
    "Why did we make this architectural decision?"
    Notice it's not primarily about how something works. It's about why you chose one approach over the alternatives.

PCM  - (Pulse Code Modulation)
    PCM is simply raw audio data.
    Instead of saying:
        "Play click.wav"
    your app loads the click into memory once
