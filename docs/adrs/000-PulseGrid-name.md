# ADR 000: Product Name — PulseGrid

Date: 2026-06-28  
Status: Accepted  

---

## Context

We are building a next-generation metronome application targeting musicians who require:

- Complex time signatures (e.g. 7/8, 13/16)
- Tempo changes (accelerando, ritardando, instant shifts)
- Timeline-based song structures
- Accent grouping and polyrhythmic practice
- High-precision audio timing (sample-accurate playback)

The former working name was functional but had limitations:

- Feels generic and tied to traditional metronome concepts
- Does not reflect timeline-based architecture
- Weak brand differentiation in App Store / Google Play
- Close to existing naming patterns (e.g. “Pro Metronome”)

We are evaluating **PulseGrid** as a potential replacement brand name.

---

## Decision Drivers

The chosen product name must:

- Be unique and not heavily saturated in the music app category
- Reflect advanced rhythm/timeline functionality
- Be short, memorable, and brandable
- Work well for App Store search and icon recognition
- Scale beyond “metronome” into a broader rhythm engine product
- Support premium positioning

---

## Considered Options

### 1. ProgMetronome
- Descriptive but generic
- Weak brand identity
- Easily confused with existing metronome apps
- Does not reflect advanced timeline system

### 2. TempoCore
- Strong “engine” feel
- Some naming saturation risk (Core-based audio tools)
- Slightly abstract for general users

### 3. Rhythm Engine
- Highly descriptive of system
- Too generic and developer-oriented
- Weak trademark and search uniqueness potential

### 4. Studio Metronome
- Clear but overly conventional
- Strong competition in naming space
- Does not reflect innovation

### 5. PulseGrid (Selected Candidate)
- Combines rhythmic concept (“Pulse”) with structural logic (“Grid”)
- Strong alignment with timeline-based architecture
- Distinctive in metronome/music app space
- Scalable into future features (AI, MIDI, analysis tools)

---

## Decision

We adopt **PulseGrid** as the official product name, pending final trademark and App Store availability verification.

---

## Consequences

### Positive

- Strong brand identity aligned with product architecture
- Clear separation from traditional metronome apps
- High memorability and visual branding potential
- Supports premium positioning and future expansion
- Flexible for extension into ecosystem products (e.g., PulseGrid Studio, PulseGrid AI)

### Negative / Risks

- Potential trademark conflicts in unrelated industries using “Pulse” or “Grid”
- Requires branding work (logo, typography, positioning) to clearly signal “music tool”
- Slight ambiguity for non-musicians without subtitle/context
- Needs App Store SEO support (“metronome”, “rhythm tool”, etc.)

---

## Mitigation Strategy

- Perform trademark search in UKIPO, EUIPO, USPTO
- Secure domain availability (pulsegrid.com or alternatives)
- Use descriptive subtitle:
  > PulseGrid — Advanced Rhythm & Timeline Metronome
- Design iconography emphasizing musical timing (not data/tech-only feel)
- Optimize App Store keywords for discoverability

---

## Outcome

**PulseGrid** is the official product name for this project.
